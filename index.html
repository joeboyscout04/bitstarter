We know that you want to spend time playing with your child, nurturing their mental and physical growth to develop into the wonderful people. We all want to feed our baby wonderful fresh, local, and homemade food but with so many daily tasks that is sometimes difficult. We are here to help! We believe that children should be able to eat the wonderful foods that we eat; starting their lives out right by eating healthy, non-processed foods.

Founded by a local foodie and child-care expert, Growing Gourmet takes the work out of feeding time by preparing healthy food fresh for your baby and delivering it to your doorstep each week. We strive to use local ingredients where possible and work with local farmers to ensure that the produce we use meets USDA Organic standards and is the freshest available. Don't settle for processed baby food in disposable pouches. If you think it tastes yucky, your baby probably will too! When your child eats Growing Gourmet not only will your child be eating local, healthy, homemade meals, but the food will be diverse and will help your child acquire a diverse palate at a young age.

FAQ

Why order from Growing Gourmet?
We use only the freshest organic ingredients, combine them into delicous and healthy baby food, and deliver it to you!
How does my purchase help the environment?
At Growing Gourmet, we take environmental protection seriously. We only work with farmers who can meet USDA organic standards, and use primarily local ingredients with a low carbon footprint. We also use reusable glass jars, so no plastic to throw away. Even your delivery is eco-friendly, with carbon offsets purchased for each delivery.
